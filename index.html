About & CV

Researcher. Psychologist.
Storyteller.

I'm a User Experience Researcher with a background in Psychology and Organisational Psychology. At Commonwealth Bank of Australia, I lead end-to-end research for product and design teams — from discovery to validation — ensuring the customer is always at the forefront of decision-making.

Over five years, I've led research for Australia's largest bank: shaping the digital identity strategy, running the Cards research stream, and delivering risk culture insights directly to the Board of Directors.

I combine rigorous qualitative methods with quantitative data to tell stories that make it easy for teams to act with confidence.
